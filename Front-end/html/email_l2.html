Lesson 2: Composing an Email

When writing an email, here’s what to remember:

  • Use a clear subject line: This helps the person know what the email is about.
  • Be polite and professional: Always use a respectful tone.
  • Sign off properly: End your emails with a polite closing, like “Best regards” or “Sincerely.”
