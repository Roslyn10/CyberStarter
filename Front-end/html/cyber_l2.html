Lesson 2: Consequences of Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying has serious mental, emotional, and sometimes physical consequences. Victims may feel:

  • Anxiety, depression, and stress.
  • A decrease in self-esteem or confidence.
  • Social withdrawal, isolation, and difficulty trusting others.
  • A decline in academic or work performance.
  • Physical symptoms like headaches, stomachaches, or sleep problems.

Cyberbullying can also have consequences for the bully:

  • Legal consequences if the bullying escalates to harassment or threats.
  • Suspension from school or expulsion from extracurricular activities.
  • Permanently damaging their reputation, which can affect future opportunities.
