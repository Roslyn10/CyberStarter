Lesson 3: Phishing Scams

Phishing refers to attempts by scammers to steal sensitive information, such as personal details, login credentials, or financial data.

There are four different types of phishing:

  • Spear Phishing: Targets specific individuals with tailored malicious emails.
  • Whaling: Targets business executives, celebrities, or people with large financial assets.
  • Smishing: Phishing attacks carried out via SMS.
  • Vishing: Phishing attacks conducted via phone calls.

When to be cautious:

  • If a website has many broken links, random pop-ups, or asks for personal information unnecessarily.
  • Be skeptical of websites offering “too good to be true” deals.
