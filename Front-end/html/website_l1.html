Lesson 1: Understanding Websites

A website is like an online book filled with information, images, videos, or content across webpages. (Think of webpages as the pages of the online book.) Websites can contain many different types of content:

  • Games
  • Blogs and social media platforms
  • Educational resources
  • Online stores
  • News outlets, etc.

Websites are accessible through internet-connected devices that support web browsing, e.g.,

  • Computers
  • Smartphones
  • Smartwatches
  • Gaming consoles
  • Smart appliances (e.g., smart cars, fridges, and TVs)
