Lesson 3: Difference Between the World Wide Web and the Internet

The internet is the infrastructure (like roads), while the World Wide Web is the information (vehicles) traveling on it.

The internet allows us access to the web; it contains the webpages. The web is just the information traveling across the internet.

The internet contains the web, but the web does not contain the internet.
